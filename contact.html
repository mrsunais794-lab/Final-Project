Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. You can view shipping options at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused products in their original packaging. Perfumes can be returned within 14 days if the seal is unbroken.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Absolutely! All LuxeGlow products are cruelty-free and never tested on animals. We are certified by Leaping Bunny.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.
