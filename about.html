Our Story

Crafting beauty since 2010

Redefining Beauty Standards

Founded in Paris in 2010, LuxeGlow began as a small boutique perfumery with a vision to create fragrances that tell a story. Our passion for authenticity and quality quickly expanded into the realm of skin care.

Today, we combine French luxury with globally-sourced natural ingredients to create products that not only enhance your appearance but also nourish your skin and elevate your spirit.

Our commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing ensures that every product we create respects both people and the planet.
